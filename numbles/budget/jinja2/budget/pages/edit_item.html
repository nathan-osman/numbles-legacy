Cron is a special format for specifying the frequency of an item. A cron entry consists of five fields, separated by a space.

[hour] [minute] [day of month] [month] [day of week]

Each field may contain a numerical value that determines which of those intervals to use or an * to use all of them.

For example, to specify "11:00am every friday", one would use:

11 0 * * 5

To specify multiples, use a / followed by the multiple. For example, to specify "the first day of every other month", one would use:

0 0 1 */2 *

You may also specify one of the following special aliases:

  • @yearly / @anually
  • @monthly
  • @weekly
  • @daily
